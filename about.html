Our Story

Bringing anime art to life, one poster at a time

How It All Started

PrintParadise began as a passion project in 2020, founded by a group of anime enthusiasts who wanted to share their love for Japanese animation and art with the world. What started as a small collection of handpicked posters has grown into a curated haven for anime art lovers.

Our journey started in a tiny apartment in Tokyo, where we spent countless hours selecting artwork that would resonate with fellow fans. Today, we're proud to offer a wide selection of high-quality anime posters, wall scrolls, and merchandise that bring your favorite characters and moments to life.
